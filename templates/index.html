Transcript Formatter

This free online tool helps you clean up transcripts by removing line numbers and timestamps. It's perfect for formatting transcripts exported from Premiere Pro or other video editing software.

How to Use

  1. Copy your transcript text (including line numbers and timestamps)
  2. Paste it into the input box below
  3. Click "Format Transcript"
  4. Copy the cleaned text from the output box

Example Input:

1 00:00:00.000 --> 00:00:02.000
Hello and welcome to our video.

2 00:00:02.000 --> 00:00:04.000
Today we'll be discussing transcript formatting.

3 00:00:04.000 --> 00:00:06.000
This tool makes it easy to clean up your text.
